Job Title: Cybersecurity Analyst

We are seeking a detail-oriented and passionate Cybersecurity Analyst to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong foundation in cybersecurity principles, risk management, and secure systems architecture. The role involves protecting sensitive information, monitoring network activity, and responding to incidents.

Responsibilities:
- Monitor and analyze security events and logs
- Conduct vulnerability assessments and penetration testing
- Implement security measures such as firewalls, encryption, and access controls
- Respond to security breaches and coordinate incident response
- Stay current with latest security threats and trends

Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Cybersecurity, or related field
- Knowledge of network security, cryptography, and secure communication protocols
- Familiarity with tools like Wireshark, Nessus, Metasploit, or similar
- Experience with Python or other scripting languages
- Understanding of SSL/TLS, authentication, and access control systems
- Certifications like CompTIA Security+, CEH, or Microsoft Security Fundamentals are a plus

Preferred Skills:
- Hands-on project experience in cybersecurity
- Strong communication and analytical skills
- Ability to work both independently and in a team
